Release Quick Tips
  • To use ACRE2, all you really need to remember is Capslock; combinations of alt, control, shift and capslock are the default keys to do everything you need.
  • You can mouse over the knobs on radios to see what they do; all radios in ACRE are usable with knobs; such as changing channels and volume.
  • If you are having trouble with the game communicating with ACRE, try running TeamSpeak and ArmA as a regular user (NOT as Administrator)
  • Radios are half duplex - this means only one person can transmit on a frequency at any time. Don't be surprised when someone hogs the net.
